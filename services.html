Our Services

We offers the full spectrum of care for every need, in a variety of settings and through community partnerships.
From primary care to urgent care, and from emergency care to trauma care, we ensure that community members receive the right care, in the right place and at the right time.

We also has the personnel and facilities to provide advanced medical inquiry and treatments. Whether the patient is coming in for a routine or a specialized procedure, they will always benefit from our unique ability to quickly implement the newest discoveries and advancements in medicine. We provide:

  • Organ transplantation
  • Trauma and Burn Care
  • Cancer Service
  • Comprehensive Neuromedicine
  • Complex Cardiology
  • Smoking cessation treatment

HealthInsider

HealthInsider is a not-for-profit independent hospital that located in Petaling Jaya. We are open to all patients every day.

We provides fundamental medical treatment and cutting-edge medicine. We commit to provide the highest quality care for every person in the community.
